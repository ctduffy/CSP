Designed specifically for the second grade team at St. Anne's Belfield School, this site uses an object oriented Javascript algorithm to generate optimal lunch table combinations. With teacher input, the students will be equally distributed to tables based on both gender and personality traits. Once the tables are generated, teachers will have the ability to manually prohibit certain combintaions of students from sitting together.
